NMRC Releases Provenance Trial Results for Ponderosa Pine Across Elevation Gradients

Five years of field data from the NMRC statewide provenance trial network reveal significant, climatically structured variation in drought tolerance and survival among ponderosa pine seed sources from across the species' New Mexico range. Results carry direct operational implications for seed collection targeting and planting prescriptions across the state's fire- and drought-affected landscapes.
